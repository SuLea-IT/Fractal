# 分形 × 植物：第一轮资料清单（2026-03-16）

## 目标期刊（暂按 NG = Nature Genetics, NP = Nature Plants 理解）
- Nature Plants aims & scope: https://www.nature.com/nplants/aims
- Nature Genetics aims & scope: https://www.nature.com/ng/aims
- Nature Plants guide to authors: https://www.nature.com/nplants/guide-to-authors
- Nature Genetics guide to authors: https://www.nature.com/ng/guide-to-authors

## 建议优先下载

### A. 分形/迭代/植物建模基础
1. The Algorithmic Beauty of Plants (Prusinkiewicz & Lindenmayer, 1990)
   - PDF: https://algorithmicbotany.org/papers/abop/abop.pdf
   - 用途：L-system、递归、植物形态生成，是“迭代”最核心的理论入口。

2. Quantitative Modeling of Plant Development / Arabidopsis development (Mündermann et al., Developmental Biology, 2005)
   - 期刊页：https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S0012160605001136
   - 用途：把 L-system 与真实植物发育数据对接。

3. Self-organizing patterning of Arabidopsis flowers: phyllotaxis and other patterns of flower arrangement on the shoot (PNAS, 2006)
   - PNAS: https://www.pnas.org/doi/10.1073/pnas.0605475103
   - 用途：说明“迭代规则 + 局部相互作用”如何产生全局图样；更偏机制，而不只是几何描述。

### B. 植物中的典型分形实例
4. A genetic network for the extreme repetition and diversity of cauliflower (Nature Genetics, 2024)
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1038/s41588-024-01696-4
   - PubMed Central: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11176064/
   - 用途：非常适合作为“自相似 + 迭代”案例，且直接对应 NG 水平。

5. Gene network dynamics govern the cauli- and romanesco-like plant shapes (Science, 2021)
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1126/science.abg5999
   - PubMed Central: https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC10027312/
   - 用途：Romanesco 的分形外观背后是发育网络与器官重复的动力学。

6. Cauliflowers: or how the perseverance of a simple cellular programme can create elaborate fractal form (J Exp Bot, 2025)
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1093/jxb/erae501
   - 用途：对 cauliflower/Romanesco 分形形态的近期综述。

### C. 其他可拓展方向：根、叶脉、网络
7. Fractal approaches to characterize the structure of herbaceous plants (Methods in Ecology and Evolution, 2024)
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1111/2041-210X.14457
   - 用途：偏方法学，适合想把“分形”做成可量化指标。

8. A modern view of the complexity of leaf venation patterns (New Phytologist, 2021)
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1111/nph.17509
   - 用途：叶脉网络的层级结构、复杂性与功能联系，可作为“准分形/层级网络”方向。

9. How well can we predict root system architecture? The role of phenotyping, genetic, and computational approaches (Plant Physiology, 2022)
   - DOI: https://doi.org/10.1093/plphys/kiac216
   - 用途：根系结构方向的综述，利于把分形量化与根系表型、功能联系起来。

## 初步判断
- 如果课题主打“植物分形长什么样”，更像科普/描述，离 NG/NP 还远。
- 如果主打“哪些发育规则/基因网络/尺度规律导致分形或准分形结构出现，并且与功能/适应度有关”，更接近 NP。
- 如果能进一步定位到明确基因、调控网络、变异来源，并解释形态重复如何产生，可更接近 NG。
